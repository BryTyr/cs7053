CS7053 security

This is the 2015 version. Last updated 20150112 by stephen.farrell@cs.tcd.ie.

  • The canonical URL for this stuff is: https://down.dsg.cs.tcd.ie/cs7053/
  • This course was previously CS7012 and before that NDS106 (the links are to the old lectures etc.) The content overlaps a good bit.
  • The Lecture slides section below has links to ppt versions of all the lectures for the course. The Materials section below has links to local copies of some of the background information/papers etc. For exam purposes, you won't need anything else other than these and easily available things like RFCs. (You are of course encouraged to read more widely).
  • You can look at some relevant old exams with both questions and answers/marking schemes.
  • There will be two assignments, totalling 20% of the marks for the module (so the exam is worth 80%).
  • Note that I may be changing the slides as I go. Depending on stuff that's more than two weeks ahead of time may be wasted effort:-)
